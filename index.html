Welcome to the website for the 2020 Software Fellow Project posters.

This year, our site visit from the National Science Foundation will be virtual. Since printed posters on the wall of MolSSI doesn't work well with a virtual visit, we are taking a different approach to software fellow posters this year.

Please contribute a "virtual poster" for this site. You will need to fork this repository and add a post. The pages are formatted using markdown. For a guide see the post "How to add your Poster" (which also shows an example of what posters will look like)
